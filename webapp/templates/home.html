Zuora's Zengage

We seek to make a huge difference with your spare change.

Zengage introduces a novel system for charity donation via paychecks. On an opt-in basis, each Zuora paycheck is rounded to the nearest dollar and the change is summed to make a donation (an average of 50 cents per 2 weeks, a dollar per month per person). Try out these new features:
  • Vote on your favorite charity once a month to help decide where your money will go.
  • Visit our dashboard to see how much money has been raised so far.
  • View your profile to see your individual contribution.
